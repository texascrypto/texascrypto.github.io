Texas Crypto

Texas Crypto is a student-run tech organization at The University of Texas at Austin with the purpose of facilitating the professional development of Blockchain technology across Austin and Texas, as well as educating the general public about the basics of Blockchain, its practical uses, and its long-term implications on both the tech industry and global economy.

How it works

Newcomers

If you or your friends are interested in Texas Crypto or just want to ask a random question, you are always welcomed. We encourage students of all different levels of experience to join or stop by.

Gurus

Even if you already know a great deal about blockchain technology, nobody knows everything! Stop by one day and share your knowledge with an interested and ambitious crowd of students, or listen to our discussion about current events. We strive to connect everyone to enhance our groups collective knowledge in hopes of doing great things with us.
