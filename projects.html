Jewelz

Jewelz is an upcoming project that will introduce a new cryptocurrency driven by a game-based platform, allowing participants to play on various apps and make in-app purchases with real cryptocurrencies! Currently in development is Jewelz diamond, the flagship cryptocurrency for the Jewelz platform, which will be able to be mined on a laptop or mobile phone! The long-term vision of this project is to gamify crypto mining and Blockchain education, which will open the door for the mass adoption of cryptocurrencies across the globe.
